Key Insights

What a decade of Jakarta's temperature data reveals

28.6°C
Decade Average
Jakarta's average temperature held steady at 28.6°C across 2015–2025, ranging from daily lows of 19.8°C to peaks of 36.8°C — a consistently tropical profile.
+0.7°C
Dry vs. Wet Season
The dry season (April–October) averages 28.9°C, while the wet season (November–March) drops to 28.2°C. October and May are the warmest months.
29.2°C
2024 — Hottest Year
2024 set the decade record at 29.15°C average, followed closely by 2023 at 29.01°C — both likely amplified by El Niño conditions.
36.8°C
All-Time Maximum
Recorded on September 29, 2023. Peak daily maximums have risen from around 35°C in 2015–2017 to over 36.5°C in recent years.
